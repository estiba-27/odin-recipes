Homemade Black Bean Veggie Burgers

Description

These black bean burgers are healthy, hearty, and packed with nutritious veggies.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables: Youâ€™ll need canned black beans, half a green bell pepper, and half an onion.
  • Seasonings: These black bean burgers are seasoned with fresh garlic, chili powder, cumin, and Thai chili sauce.
  • Egg: A whole egg keeps the burgers from drying out and helps bind the patties together.

Steps

    Steps

  1. Mash the beans and chop the remaining vegetables and garlic in the food processor.
  2. Stir the veggies into the beans and combine the seasonings and egg in a separate bowl.
  3. Mix in the bread crumbs and divide the mixture into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for about 8 minutes on each side.
