Avocado Breakfast Sandwich

Description

Make this egg and avocado sandwich for a delicious restaurant-style breakfast at home! It's quick, easy, and delicious.
The sliced avocado is what makes this sandwich pop and gives it a unique flavor.
It is chock-full of protein, as well, so this sandwich will keep you full until lunchtime.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • 2 slices Italian bread
  • 1 ounce Swiss cheese, sliced
  • 1 ounce Cheddar cheese, sliced
  • Â½ ripe avocado, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Crack eggs into skillet;
  3. Spread butter onto 1 side of each bread slice.
  4. Carefully flip sandwich to grill other side until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Remove sandwich from skillet and cut in half.
